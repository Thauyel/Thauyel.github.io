Selected Projects

Project 01

Apollo

A comprehensive cultural arts center designed for Bagdat Street, serving as a community hub for artistic expression and cultural exchange. The project features modular sustainable architecture with exhibition halls, multipurpose studios, workshops, and performance spaces. Designed to accommodate diverse user groups including art enthusiasts, collectors, creators, and the curious public, while promoting local artists and providing accessible cultural programming for the neighborhood.

Project 02

Tremora

A safe haven in times of need, a community hub every day. This project aims deliver a relief home structure to support a modular assembly system. The building's core components are designed for rapid assembly and disassembly, allowing for an efficient transformation from a community center to an emergency response hub. The modular units can be used simultaneously from world gatherings to crisis response, disaster recovery, and relief efforts as community-centric response scenarios.

Project 03

Hermes

Located within the confines of a former quarry, Hermes reinterprets an artificial landscape through deliberate architectural intervention. The design accepts the site's post-industrial condition and engages in active reshaping, introducing a dual-axis organizational strategy that guides visitors through open recreational programs while accommodating controlled-access functions. The modular structure of steel and sandwich panels extends downward, adapting to the topographic slope and transforming the quarry into a dynamic recreational hub. Rather than merely occupying the space, the building dialogues with the evolving landscape, serving as a constructed memory that mediates between the visible and hidden, the active and contemplative.
